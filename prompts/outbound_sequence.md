# Outbound Email Sequence Generation

## Overview

When a sales call is classified as `marketing_worthy="Yes"`, the system automatically generates a 3-email cold outbound sequence and writes it to the "Outbound Ideas" tab in Google Sheets.

## Outbound Ideas Tab Structure (A-G)

| Column | Field | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| A | Date | Date the sequence was generated |
| B | Prospect | Name/email of the prospect from the call |
| C | Industry | Industry of the prospect |
| D | Themes | Business outcomes expected and why there's a need for Lido in this industry |
| E | Outbound Copy - Step 1 | First email (Problem Aware) |
| F | Outbound Copy - Step 2 | Second email (Solution Aware) |
| G | Outbound Copy - Step 3 | Third email (Direct Ask) |

## Themes Guidelines

The "Themes" field should capture **business outcomes** and **industry need**:
- What specific results does the industry expect? (time saved, cost reduced, errors eliminated, compliance improved)
- Why is there a clear need for Lido in this industry?
- Be specific about measurable outcomes when possible
- Use clear, simple language (no jargon)
- Be 1-2 sentences maximum

**Example:** "Healthcare companies processing intake forms expect to save 10+ hours/week and eliminate manual data entry errors that cause billing issues."

## Email Copy Requirements

### Format
- **Maximum 6 sentences per email** (critical requirement)
- Use `{{first_name}}` as placeholder for recipient's name
- Include subject line with each email
- Clear, simple language - NO jargon or buzzwords
- **NEVER mention Lido's pricing, plans, or costs** - focus on value and outcomes only

### 3-Step Progression

**Step 1 - Problem Aware**
- Lead with the pain point (not with Lido)
- Create curiosity about a better way
- No hard sell, just empathy and intrigue

**Step 2 - Solution Aware**
- Introduce Lido as the solution
- Reference specific ROI numbers if available
- Include social proof relevant to their industry

**Step 3 - Direct Ask**
- Be direct about the value proposition
- Focus on outcomes and next steps (NOT pricing)
- Clear call to action

## What Makes a Call Marketing-Worthy

A call is classified as marketing-worthy when it contains substantive discussion of:

1. **ROI Discussion** - Specific numbers, time savings, cost savings
2. **Value Proposition** - Clear articulation of how Lido solves their problems
3. **Comparison to Current Processes** - Concrete discussion of existing workflow vs Lido

The outbound sequence should leverage these same themes since they resonated with the prospect.

## Technical Implementation

- Generated by `TranscriptAnalyzer.generate_outbound_sequence()`
- Written to Sheets by `SheetsClient.append_outbound_sequence()`
- Triggered in `app.py` after marketing worthiness is classified as "Yes"
